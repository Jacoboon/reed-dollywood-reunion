Why Dollywood? Why Now?

As kids, we gathered at Uncle Steve’s house in Ohio for holidays and big family moments. Life got busy, people spread out, and those traditions faded into “remember when…?”

This site is our way to bring the Reed tribe back together in one place: Dollywood in late spring, when the park is covered in flowers and the Smoky Mountains are green and alive.

Friends, partners, kids, and bonus family are all welcome. If you love a Reed, you’re invited.

Pricing Overview (Approximate)

These are ballpark numbers so everyone can plan a budget. Final prices may change slightly when we book.

Park Tickets

  • Adult 2-Day: ~$134 per person
  • Adult 1-Day: ~$101 per person
  • Kids / Seniors: A bit cheaper (we’ll share exact numbers when we book)

Food in the Park

  • Plan on: ~$40–50 per person per park day
  • Covers 2 meals + snacks
  • Outside the park, we’ll use the cabin kitchen to save money

Parking & Other

  • Parking at Dollywood: ~$25 per car per day
  • Cabin, groceries, and special extras like Dolly Parton’s Stampede will be handled separately.

Event Details (Draft)

These details are flexible and will be finalized once we see everyone’s RSVPs.

When

Target window: late April to early May, during Dollywood’s Flower & Food Festival (late April–early June each year).

Where

Dollywood theme park in Pigeon Forge, Tennessee, plus a nearby cabin or hotel for the Reed tribe to gather.

Who’s Invited?

All Reeds, their people, and anyone else who feels like family. Partners, kids, friends — if you love us, you’re in.

What’s the Plan?

  • 3 nights in a cabin or hotel
  • 2 days in Dollywood (rides, shows, flowers, food)
  • One big shared dinner and memory night
  • Optional group outing like Dolly Parton’s Stampede
